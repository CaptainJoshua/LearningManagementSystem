Mission

To develop the intelligence of the young ones by providing them with quality and affordable education, enhance their well-being thru values formation and to harness their respective skills and talents in a manner of effective and efficient methods given by the management and staff.

Vision

An institution whose core values are perseverance, competence, honesty and integrity. Imbued among its employees and staff the virtues of professionalism and excellence in dispensing their duties and responsibilities. Where children are nurtured with love and respect and rearing them to become a well-learned and a more responsible citizen of the community. Above all, to be morally and spiritually fit in the Christian world.

Mary JV's School History

Mary JV' School Inc. has its humble beginnings so to speak. It did not have the grand and lavish opening like the others. Nor did it dream to become like what is today. It is basically the fruit of labor purge through dedication, patience, and perseverance, care and love yto young children and above all, faith in God.....

Mary JV'S School started as JV'S Tutorial and Educational Center in 2004, catering to the call of parents who saw that their children need some tutoring and tapped the help of their neighbour. Mrs. Josephine D. Vito, who readily extended her assistance. During that time, there were only six little kids being tutored by Teacher Jo. These young ones made the difference in catapulting the name of JV'S in the community. And soon after, its reputation become a word of mouth to being parent.

Today, Mary JV'S School Inc. is a very promising institution. It is committed in nurturing every child into becoming creative, talented, God fearing and responsible citizen of the community, in particular and the country in general. Because at Mary JV'S School Inc. we continuously strive to become better from best.
